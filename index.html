Day 1

Pickup at Tirupati, proceed for darshan of Srinivasa Managapuram (Old Balaji Temple). Head to Kanipakam to visit Sri Varasidhi Vinayaka Swamy. Proceed to Siripuram near Vellore for darshan of Sri Lakshmi Narayani (Golden Temple). Drive to Tiruvannamalai (Arunachalam), darshan of Lord Arunachaleswarar. Overnight stay in Tiruvannamalai or Kancheepuram.

Day 2

In morning, drive to Kancheepuram. Visit famous temples: Ekambareswarar, Kailasanathar, Varadaraja Perumal and Kamakshi Amman. Head to Tiruttani to visit Sri Murugan (Karttikeya). Drive back to Tirupati.
